# Frecuently Asked Questions

## What is Cinemapi?

Cinemapi is a cinema aggregator. It gathers **public** information from many cinema websites, classifies and organizes it, and displays it with filters for users.

## The ticket price on the cinema website is different.

Each cinema has its own pricing system. Some cinemas have different prices depending on the day of the week. Others even use dynamic pricing based on demand, purchase time, availability, etc.

Currently, Cinemapi only supports two prices:
- standard price
- discounted price (spectator day)

In the future, we plan to expand this system to support more pricing variations.

However, Cinemapi will never support dynamic pricing, promotions, or temporary offers. You should **always** check the original cinema website to ensure the information is accurate.

## The showtimes do not match the cinema website.

The original website may have updated its schedule and Cinemapi has not refreshed the data yet. It is also possible that there is an issue in our system.

Please report any issue to: ayuda@cinemapi.es

## Some movies are missing from a cinema.

Movie listings are updated every 5 days. A cinema may have recently added a new movie that has not yet been synchronized with Cinemapi.

## I found an error. How can I report it?

Thank you for helping improve Cinemapi.

Please contact us at: ayuda@cinemapi.es

## Does Cinemapi sell tickets?

No. Cinemapi only aggregates and displays public cinema information. Ticket purchases are always completed on the original cinema website.

## Where does Cinemapi get its information from?

All information comes from publicly available cinema websites. Cinemapi does not create or modify movie schedules or cinema data manually.

## Does Cinemapi offer refunds?

No. Cinemapi does not sell tickets or any other product. Ticket purchases, and possible refunds are always completed on the original cinema website.
